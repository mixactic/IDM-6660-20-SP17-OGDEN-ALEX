Audio

Alex has been making original electronic music since 1998. Formerly one half of the electronic music project, M45, she currently creates under the alias hoof.beat, forging a signature sound that incorporates elements from the experimental, ambient, electronica, IDM, and drum 'n' bass genres.

Bridging the divide between anaolgue and digital realms, Alex's compositions combine warmer refrains from acoustic guitar and recorded ambient noise with cooler textures from MIDI-based instruments, computer synthesizers, and punchy drum machine rhythms.

Hear hoof.beat on Soundcloud.

Web

Alex fell in love with coding after learning BASIC in grade school in 1990, and has been tinkering with HTML and CSS since 1997. She is passionate about writing quality code, and enjoys pushing the boundaries of web development, experimenting with UX and UI, and learning as much as she can about all things Internet. Alex is currently a student in MCAD's Interactive Design and Marketing program.

Connect with Alex on GitHub.
